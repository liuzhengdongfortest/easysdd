## Core timeline
- 1967: Born in London.
- 1980s: Studies industrial design at Newcastle Polytechnic.
- Early 1990s: Works at Tangerine on broad product classes.
- 1992: Joins Apple full-time in Cupertino.
- 1997-1998: Senior design leadership and iMac era inflection.
- 2001-2010: iPod, iPhone, iPad era; hardware identity consolidation.
- 2012: Given Human Interface leadership in addition to industrial design responsibilities.
- 2015: Named Apple Chief Design Officer.
- 2017: Returns to direct responsibility for product design.
- 2019: Announces departure from Apple employment and formation of LoveFrom with Apple as client.
